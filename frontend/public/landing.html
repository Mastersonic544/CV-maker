The Process
Three phases.
Zero manual effort.
01
Discover & Research

Feed your profile once. AI suggests roles, scrapes LinkedIn for live listings, then deep-dives each company — CEO posts, HR signals, cultural values — to synthesize a hiring persona for every target.

OpenRouter · Playwright
02
Generate & Score

Two LLM instances go to war. One generates your CV, the other scores it as a ruthless HR critic. The loop runs until you hit 9+/10. A completely different, optimized document per company — never reused.

GAN Loop · Llama 3.3 70B
03
Apply & Practice

The queue sends via LinkedIn Easy Apply, Brevo SMTP, and LinkedIn DMs with human-like timing. The interview simulator then role-plays as your exact HR contact so you arrive knowing the answers.
